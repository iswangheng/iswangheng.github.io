Why Do I Need A Professional Email Signature

- They’re the blocks at the end of the communications you receive from colleagues and everybody else looking to connect with you professionally that have their name and contact information

- A professional email signature is more than just your name—it’s all of your contact information stacked together at a glance

- By providing all of this, your email signature makes it incredibly easy for your email’s recipient to get back to you in the most efficient way possible, whether that’s a phone call, a LinkedIn connection, scheduling a meeting through Calendly, or simply replying to the email

- By doing all of that, a well-crafted professional email signature increases the likelihood of your email converting
