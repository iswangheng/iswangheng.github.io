## Strategy

- is commonly referred to as an Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- just a basic formula:

We help [company type] with [defining attributes] who need [job to be done] to [core outcome]. That doesn't require a long winded process

- A few examples
We help B2B SaaS startups with fewer than 50 employees who need more pipeline without a business development team to consistently book meetings

- We help UK consultancies serving FinTech who need case-study proof to win Series A clients and shorten revenue teams cycles

- We help boutique tech recruiters placing first RevOps hires at seed-stage SaaS to make three strategic placements per quarter

- Be specific—add attributes like size, industry, location, or tech used
